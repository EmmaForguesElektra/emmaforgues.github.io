carcassesSolubles, A/V performance, 2018


carcassesSolubles exhibits the duality existing between the metaphysical, spiritual and statistical nature of the human being and his embodied, mortal and material one. From this first posture results the Anthropocene, referring to humanity as the main actor in the recent environmental changes. Opposed to this human-centric point of view, the artists use their bodies as a bond between their own existence and the Earth, and project on them a visual which accounts for urbanization, migration, epidemics, information overload, and exploitation of natural resources.
