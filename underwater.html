UnderWater, video installation, 2015 04:55

UnderWater is a projection interacting with a drawing. I experimented with the expressive design language by establishing a link between a still image and a projected moving image. During the video, there are more sequences of the body which floats in the water that correspond to a drawing which represents 6 trajectories of the subjectâ€™s body.
