she.PHASE

she.PHASE is a collaboration between Emma Forgues and Sam Bourgault. Both studying Computation Arts, their heterogenous experience, from art to science, leads to a diversified approach in their artistic practice. Their common interests involve the body, cybernetics, interactivity, both in the real and in the virtual world.

As she.PHASE, they have performed in multiple events in Montreal: MUTEK (2017), InnerEchoes (2017, 2018), VAULT (2018-2020), LIP (2018), Algorave (2018), Slut Island Festival (2019), Suoni Per Il Popolo (2019) and more.
